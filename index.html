The Path Through the House

  1. Enter through the front door
  2. Walk into the living room
  3. Follow the smell of food into the kitchen
  4. Move through the hallway
  5. Go upstairs toward the bedrooms

Front Door

The front door is the first step into the house. It feels like a quiet entrance into memories that have been waiting behind it.

Living Room

The living room is where voices, laughter, and family moments gather. It feels like the heart of the house.

Kitchen

The kitchen is filled with warmth, familiar smells, and the comfort of meals being made while conversations happen in the background.

Hallway

The hallway connects every room, but it also makes the house feel like a maze of different memories.

Stairs

The stairs lead from one level of the house to another, like moving between different times in my life.

Bedroom

The bedroom keeps private memories, quiet thoughts, and small objects that remind me of childhood.

Office Room

The office room holds papers, plans, schoolwork, and dreams for the future.

Bathroom

The bathroom mirror shows reflections of the past and small moments of getting ready for the day.

Window

The window looks out at the world beyond the house, while still reflecting what is inside.
